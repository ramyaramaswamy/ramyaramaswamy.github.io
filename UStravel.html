Why do we do this

Sanath and I love to travel and explore new places. It lets us open our eyes and discover who we are. It is extraordinary to see both natural and man-made wonders. We become more aware of the sun and the moon that follows us wherever we go. We step out from our laptop screens to smell fresh air, friendly people and good food. We live our life just once. We want to make it happy and memorable doing things that matter to us!
